ABOUT

Lord of the Drinks, commonly known as LOTD, has become one of the most sought-after places in cities like Delhi, Mumbai, chenani, Bengaluru & Pune. LOTD is a home to the tastiest food and eccentric drinks. Lord Of The Drinks founded by restaurateur Priyank Sukhija. Opened its doors for Delhi-wallahs in the year 2015, the restro-lounge is spread across two floors with huge criss-cross windows overlooking the bustling Connaught Place. If you like the drinks and metaphors mixed, you’ll enjoy the amazing medieval chaos that is offered by Lord of the Drinks.The vibe at Lord of the Drinks is distinctly antique, intimate and yet hopping.
