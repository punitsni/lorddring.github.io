ABOUT

Salutations from Lord of the Drinks, commonly known as LOTD, best restaurant and bar and your favorite place to hang out. Welcome to the land of taste as we take you on the best incomparable best Dining restaurant experience with an array of lip-smacking food and art.

Lord of the Drinks serves you the best of bending food curated from local and imported ingredients for all the food lovers out there. Our commitment to our patrons stays strong for serving the best food coupled with an exhilarating concept ambiance set in the medieval era theme. Step in and experience the Lord of the Drink’s vibes.

man behindlord of the drinks

Among the most prolific restaurateurs in the country, Priyank Sukhija started his journey at the age of 19, with capital from his father, he set up a restaurant (Lazeez Affaire in New Delhi’s Malcha Marg) to foster his entrepreneurial zeal. Twenty years on, Lazeez Affaire is thriving in an industry that sees fast churn, and Sukhija’s portfolio at the First Fiddle Pvt Ltd has expanded to 15 brands including Lord of the Drinks, Tamasha and Plum By Bent Chair, Dragonfly Experience) with 35+ outlets any counting across four cities. Talking about the capital New Delhi arguably has the best food scene in India. Lots of new, experimental venues have cropped up in the last five years, and they provide stiff competition to the few long-standing restaurants that have survived the test. His current tally stands at 27, the maverick may have opened at least a couple more. Among them his successful outlet chain.
